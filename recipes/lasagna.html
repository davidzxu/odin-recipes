Lasagna Recipe

Description

A rich and creamy pasta dish filled layer by layer with refreshingly fresh onions and garlic, lathered in a succulent meat sauce and topped with imported, premium quality mozzarella.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 0.75 pounds lean ground beef
  • 0.5 cups minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 28 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 6.5 ounces tomato sauce
  • 0.5 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1.5 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 0.5 teaspoons fennel seeds
  • 0.25 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 0.75 pounds mozzarella cheese
  • 0.75 cups grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 Â½ hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 0.5 teaspoons salt.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  6. To assemble, spread meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with half of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with a third of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  7. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  9. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
